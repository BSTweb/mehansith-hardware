Mehansith Hardware Stores

Our Story: Our journey began in 1992 when Mr. P.A.A Perera opened the doors of Mehansith Hardware with a vision to provide our community with the best tools, materials, and expert advice. Over the years, we've grown from a small shop into a thriving hardware store, thanks to the loyalty and support of our customers. We're more than just a store we're your go-to destination for all things hardware and home improvement. Our commitment to quality, service, and community has been the cornerstone of our business for more than 20 years.

Our Mission: At Mehansith Hardware, our mission is simple: to empower DIY enthusiasts, homeowners, and professionals by providing them with the highest quality products and the knowledge to use them effectively. We believe that every project, big or small, deserves the right tools and materials.
